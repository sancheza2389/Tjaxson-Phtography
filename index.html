A little about me..

I’m always up for an adventure, but also can’t wait to lay on the couch and watch a couple of movies with a box of pizza. I can get lost in a book for hours with a nice large bowl of cookies and cream ice cream next to me, or scroll through Pintrest ( yes I'm obssessed ) pinning crafts and recipes to try out when I'm not exhausted from chasing my son around.

I love capturing people in those real moments, a quick glance at a loved one, a spontaneous tickle fight, or silly faces. Being able to have clients feel the love when they look back on photos is what makes it all worth it. Ready to have a great time?
